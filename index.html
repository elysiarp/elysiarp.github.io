Elysia Roleplay Community Rules

Read all rules carefully to understand what is expected both in-city and within our Discord.

Discord Link: Join Elysia Roleplay

Last Updated: 09/25/23

General Provisions

  • Change of Rules: All rules are subject to change at the discretion of staff.
  • Enforcement: Staff reserve the right to determine when and where these rules have been violated.

Community Standards

  • Bullying/Harassing: Continuous rude comments or making indirect comments to subtly hurt someone is prohibited.
  • Derogatory Terms: Includes homophobia, racism, transphobia, sexism, hate speech, and the use of derogatory terms such as the N-word or "retard."
  • Toxicity: In-city disputes must not be discussed in Discord. Creating a toxic environment through actions or reactions is grounds for removal.
  • Threatening Behavior: Threats of harm, reputation damage, staff manipulation, personal information leaks, or DDOS attacks will not be tolerated and may involve law enforcement.
  • Doxxing: Releasing any personal information without permission is strictly prohibited.
  • Hack-usations: Accusations of cheating must be reported to staff privately.
  • Staff Impersonation: Impersonating staff by name or behavior is prohibited.
  • Honesty to Staff: Always be truthful when questioned by staff about potential rule violations.

18+ Community

  • Age Requirement: Players must be 18 or older, following the ESRB rating for GTA.

Discord Communication

  • In-Game Communication: Use city voice chat for all in-game interactions; external Discord communication about in-city information is not allowed.
  • Private Channels: Each gang will have private channels. Creating separate Discord channels for gangs is not permitted.

Reporting

  • Incident Reporting: If you witness a rule violation, report it with evidence. Retaliatory reports and false accusations are not tolerated.

In-City Conduct

  • Account Responsibility: You are solely responsible for your account's security and any actions taken.
  • Working Microphone: A working microphone with push-to-talk enabled is required.
  • Exploits and Bugs: Using any known exploits or bugs for advantage is prohibited.
  • Modifications: Modifications that provide an unfair advantage are not allowed (e.g., tracers, external crosshairs).
  • Green Zones: No criminal activity is allowed in designated green zones like Alta Apartments lobby.
  • Streaming: Do not use information from streams in-game. Blind your stream when accessing illegal locations.
  • Bodycam and Clipping: Using bodycam footage to gain in-game information is not allowed unless it's part of a roleplayed scenario.

Character and Roleplay

  • Character Names: Must be realistic and not contain offensive or famous real-life names.
  • Character Creation: Customize your character's appearance when joining the server for the first time.
  • Roleplay Integrity: Engage in high-quality roleplay. Low effort roleplay, like motiveless shootouts or disruptive behavior, is discouraged.
  • Life Valuation: Always value your character's life in threatening situations.
  • New Life Rule: Forget the circumstances of your character's death and preceding events unless reminded under permissible scenarios.
  • Permanent Death: High-quality roleplay is required for character executions, which must be agreed upon beforehand.

Combat and Robbery

  • Engagement Rules: Interactions that lead to combat or robbery must be initiated with proper roleplay.
  • Hostage and Kidnapping: Hostages must be used appropriately within scenes, and friends should not be used as stand-ins for genuine kidnapping scenarios.

Group and Gang Conduct

  • Group Limits: A maximum of 6 participants in criminal activities, except in specified scenarios like home base defenses.
  • Gang Rules: Up to 15 members per gang, with strict adherence to roleplay standards and wardrobe guidelines during activities.
