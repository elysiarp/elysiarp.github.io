Elysia Roleplay Community Rules

Introduction

Welcome to the Elysia Roleplay Community! It's essential to familiarize yourself with these rules to ensure a harmonious environment both in-city and within our Discord.

General Provisions

  • All rules are subject to change at the discretion of the staff.
  • Staff reserve the right to enforce and interpret these rules as needed.

Community Standards

  • No Bullying or Harassment: Continuous rude or upsetting comments, indirect insults, slandering, or creating rumors are prohibited.
  • No Derogatory Language: Including but not limited to homophobia, racism, transphobia, sexism, hate speech (e.g., use of the N-word, 'retard').
